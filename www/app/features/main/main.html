How it Works

TREE View helps you discover where chemical releases are coming from in your local community by using the latest information from the EPA’s Toxics Release Inventory and Superfund Program.

This tool is designed to let you filter this information by chemical type, related health effects, which facility these chemicals are coming from, and many other filtering options to optimize your results.
