How it Works

Step One

Enter a zip code or county to see chemical emissions in that area.

Step Two

Filter your results by toggling between chemical types, health effects and facilities in the sidebar.

Step Three

Share your experience by clicking the "Share" button!

TREEVIEW helps you discover where chemical emissions are coming from in your local community using the latest information from the EPA's Toxic Release Inventory and Superfund Program.
This tool is designed to let you filter by chemical type, related health effects, which facility the chemicals are coming from, and many other filtering options to optimize your results.
